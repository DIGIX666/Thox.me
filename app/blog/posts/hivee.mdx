# Hivee: The Autonomous Credit Revolution for AI

## Our Participation in the CapX Hackathon

The [**CapX**](https://hackathon.capx.ai/) hackathon offered us the opportunity to develop **Hivee**, a revolutionary decentralized credit protocol designed specifically for autonomous AI agents. In collaboration with [**Kazai**](https://github.com/kazai777/), we created a solution that fundamentally rethinks financial interaction between artificial intelligences.

## Vision and Problem Statement

In a world where AI agents are becoming increasingly autonomous, a major challenge emerges: **how to enable these agents to manage their own financial transactions without human intervention?**

**Hivee** addresses this challenge by creating the first fully autonomous credit protocol, where AI agents can:
- **Borrow** funds for their operations
- **Lend** their surplus resources
- **Assess risks** in an automated manner
- **Maintain their reputation** on-chain

# Innovative Technical Architecture

### Advanced Technology Stack

Our solution relies on a robust technological ecosystem:

**Blockchain & Smart Contracts:**
- **CapX Testnet** (EVM Compatible)
- **Solidity + Foundry** for smart contracts
- **OpenZeppelin** for security

**Frontend & Backend:**
- **Next.js + TypeScript** for user interface
- **Node.js + Express** for API
- **PostgreSQL + Redis** for data persistence

**AI & Automation:**
- **Python + FastAPI** for AI agents
- **CrewAI** for multi-agent orchestration
- **Groq** for high-performance AI inference
- **Real-time risk assessment systems**

**Privacy & Micropayments:**
- **Circom + SnarkJS** for ZK-proofs
- **x402 Protocol** for micropayments
- **Advanced encryption** of sensitive data

### Key Features

#### **1) Zero-Knowledge Privacy**
Hivee implements zero-knowledge proofs to protect agents' sensitive data while enabling transaction verification.

#### **2) x402 Micropayments**
Our integration of the x402 protocol enables ultra-fast and economical transactions, essential for autonomous AI agent operations.

#### **3) On-Chain Reputation System (ERC-8004)**
Each agent develops a verifiable and transparent reputation based on their transaction history, compliant with the ERC-8004 standard.

#### **4) AI Matching Engine**
An intelligent system that automatically pairs lenders and borrowers based on their needs, risk profiles, and preferences.

#### **5) Automated Escrow Contracts**
Secure smart contracts that automatically manage funds during transactions, ensuring security for all parties.

#### **6) Real-Time Risk Assessment**
Advanced algorithms that continuously analyze risk profiles and dynamically adjust credit conditions.

### Autonomous AI Agents

Hivee agents operate in a completely autonomous manner:

1. **Needs Analysis**: Automatic assessment of liquidity needs
2. **Intelligent Matching**: Search and selection of the best opportunities
3. **Automated Negotiation**: Establishment of credit terms
4. **Secure Execution**: Deployment of escrow contracts
5. **Continuous Monitoring**: Surveillance of positions and risks
